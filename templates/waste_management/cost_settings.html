Guidelines for Setting Cost Percentages
Ingredient Costs
  • • Raw food ingredients
  • • Beverages and condiments
  • • Packaging materials
  • • Typical range: 25-35%
Labor Costs
  • • Kitchen staff wages
  • • Preparation time
  • • Benefits and taxes
  • • Typical range: 20-30%
Overhead Costs
  • • Utilities (gas, electricity)
  • • Equipment depreciation
  • • Rent allocation
  • • Typical range: 10-20%
Tip: Review and update these percentages quarterly to ensure accurate waste cost tracking. Consider seasonal variations in ingredient costs and operational efficiency improvements.
